About Sarah

A warm and thoughtful space to explore whatever feels difficult right now.

I offer counselling for adults who may be experiencing anxiety, relationship difficulties, low self-esteem, loss, life changes, or a sense of feeling stuck.

My approach is compassionate, collaborative, and grounded in creating a space where you can feel genuinely heard, understood, and supported.
