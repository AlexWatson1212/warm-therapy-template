What can be customised?

Everything can be shaped around your practice.

Name & logo

Replace the demo identity with your real practice name, logo and visual feel.

Images

Use your own portrait, therapy room, nature imagery or carefully chosen placeholders.

Wording

Rewrite the copy around your voice, approach, values and client group.

Colours

Adjust the palette so the website feels softer, warmer, bolder or more minimal.

Sections

Add or remove areas such as fees, FAQs, supervision, online work or availability.

Contact flow

Connect the site to your email, enquiry form, booking page or existing process.

How the template becomes yours

Simple, thoughtful, collaborative.

We begin with the feel of the site, then shape the words, images and layout around the real person or practice behind it.

The aim is not to create something generic, but to use this template as a calm starting point for a website that feels grounded and personal.
