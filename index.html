A little about me.

I'm a qualified counsellor with experience supporting adults through anxiety, grief, relationship difficulties and periods of significant change.

My approach is thoughtful and unhurried. I won't rush you toward answers or tell you how you should feel. We'll explore things together, at a pace that feels manageable. However tangled things seem, you don't have to arrive with the right words. Bringing the muddle is enough.
